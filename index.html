About CreeperHUB.com

A short note on what's happening.

CreeperHUB.com started as a fun sandbox, a place to host all of my side projects, random experiments, and internal tools. What began as a hobbyist side project has grown organically over time. My fault...

However, I've realized I want to turn it into much more. As the range of services I want to offer to people continues to expand, the infrastructure hosted here is evolving into a critical part of a larger network.

Because of this growth, I've realized that the "Creeper" name might not be suitable enough to represent the professional scope of what I am building. If I plan to register this as a company in the future, the identity needs to reflect the creativity, utility, and reliability of the services provided. It has to be compliant with trademarks as well, I don't want Microsoft to get mad :).

That is why I am slowly transitioning the umbrella brand to Creativitatis. While the CreeperHUB domains will be available for a while, the future belongs to a more professional identity.

— Giocoliere
